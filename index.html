Blitz Privacy Policy

>

Blitz takes your data and privacy rights very seriously. While in use, Blitz may request access to your device’s camera, contacts, photo library, push notifications, or location as a way to upload profile pictures, invite other users to the app, or provide accurate search results. You are free to reject or retract these permissions at any time. Blitz may also store user email addresses to facilitate account creation, login processes, and user authentication. Blitz does not share any user information with any third party entities. Blitz aims to protect your personal information through a system of organizational and technical security measures. You may review, change, or terminate your account at any time. Upon your request to terminate your account, the account, as well as information from Blitz’s active database will be deactivated or deleted. For more information, contact info.blitzapp@gmail.com.
